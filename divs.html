divs

HTML Division (div) is a container element and it is used to group related items together.

What behavior do divs have?

None. They are only containers.

When do we use divs?

When we want to position a group of elements together.

 
								
									
								
							
								
									

										
What do we do with a div?

										
With the div tag, you can group large sections of HTML elements together and format them with CSS.
